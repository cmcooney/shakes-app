Philo4 Shakespeare's Plays Reader
ARTFL Project, University of Chicago

Philo4 Shakespeare's Plays is a version of PhiloReader that interacts with the PhiloLogic4 build of Shakespeare's Plays (data developed by the MONK project) on the ARTFL Project's servers at the University of Chicago. The original text data can be obtained at:

http://quest.library.illinois.edu/monk/project/downloads/.

With this app, you can do simple word or bibliographic searching and, from search results, link to larger text sections. For more details, touch the information icon at the upper right portion of the screen when the app is open.

To begin searching, touch below. When app is open, touch the icon in the upper left portion of the action bar to toggle open the search drawer.
